Roles and Permissions

Overview

There are two major permissions groups, Student and Employee. The Instructor and Counselor groups are sub-groups of the Employee group. This allows the Instructor and Counselor groups to both have all of the permissions of the Employee group, in addition to a few special permissions of their own. The table below illustrates the available menu options, as well as actions, each group has permission to use. Please note that only Counselors have the ability to mark a 'Note' and a 'Visit' as private.
